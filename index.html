Research Interests

Computer Vision Deep Learning Deepfake Detection & Generation Medical Image Analysis Vision Transformers Generative Adversarial Networks Multimodal Sentiment Analysis Smart IoT Systems Network Security & Cryptography Image & Video Processing

My research group works on developing deep neural architectures and pattern recognition frameworks for real-world problems. Current active projects include: Classifier Posterior GANs for deepfake synthesis & forensics, Vision Transformer-based lung cancer detection from CT scans, and multimodal audio-visual sentiment analysis. I collaborate with faculty at NIT Agartala, Tripura University, and international researchers.
