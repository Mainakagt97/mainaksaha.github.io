About Me

Assistant Professor & Passionate Researcher

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Techno College of Engineering Agartala. Parallelly, I am pursuing my PhD in CSE at the prestigious National Institute of Technology Agartala.

Over the past several years, I have taught core subjects in Computer Science and Engineering while dedicating my research activities to solving real-world challenges using Artificial Intelligence. My studies cover deep learning architectures, multimodal sentiment analysis, medical imaging, and generative adversarial networks (GANs).

Beyond research and teaching, I actively coordinate tech fests, summer internships, hackathons, and webinars, helping to bridge the gap between academic theory and industry applications.
