About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Techno College of Engineering Agartala (TCEA) and a PhD Scholar at the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Agartala. My research lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning, with a focus on real-world healthcare and security applications.

I have authored 15+ publications in peer-reviewed international journals and conferences (IEEE, CRC Press), hold a granted Indian patent on an AI-based learning disability detection device, and co-authored a textbook on Machine Learning. I am a member of IAENG and the Internet Society.
